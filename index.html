How the Revolution Works ```

We make a way through all the noise. We promote movies with our voices, our towns, our nights out. And when our voice gets loud enough — theaters will come to us. They will ask to partner with us to bring new films to town.

Ideas and stories no one has seen. Films that never got a chance. We make a pathway for every film to be seen — and for every filmmaker to be heard.

01
The Event Begins
Live indie rock band. Local comedians doing a themed set. Costumed actors roaming the crowd serving drinks and taking photos. The room is alive before the first frame rolls.
02
The Film Screens
An indie film nobody has seen — one that never got a meeting in Hollywood. Watched together, in the same room, with no distractions. This is why everyone came.
03
You Stay After
Local bar or venue. Renegades stay. They talk about the film, the band, the night. They make friends. They come back next tour. That is how we change everything.

"A live band. Comedians. Actors in costume. Then the lights go down and a film plays that Hollywood ignored. Our voice will be so loud that theaters come to us — asking to partner, asking to bring new films to town."

The Story

Indie Revolutions was built because the system was never built for the little guy. Independent filmmakers pour everything into their work — and then watch it die on a hard drive because they can't get a meeting in Hollywood.

We built a platform that matches indie films with the people in every American town who are hungry for exactly what those filmmakers made. No studios. No algorithms. Just cinema finding its people.

🎬 The Renegade Code

There is
one rule.

We talk about movies and the positive things they bring into our lives. Nothing more. Nothing less.

The world outside is a minefield of negativity, judgement, and noise. Leave it at the door.

"We are warriors — for film, for culture, for each other. Every creed. Every background. Every person who has ever felt like the world wrote them off."

We don't judge. We don't tear down. We lift up. If you've fallen — from grace, from hope, from belonging — there is a seat for you in the dark next to us.

We are Renegades.
All of us.
Together.

SCENE / THE REAL REVOLUTION 🎬 Our Mission

This isn't about ratings.
It's about people.

```

No Rotten Tomatoes. No thumbs up or thumbs down. No algorithm telling you what's good. Just people watching movies together and talking about what it meant to them.

"This is about us watching movies and hanging out. About making new friends and being a friend. We make paths for each other."

After the film — or before it — you grab a beer or a coffee. You take over a local bar, a diner, a coffee shop. Not in a bad way. In the best way. You bring life to places that need it. You spend money in your town. You talk to strangers who become friends.

That is what Indie Revolutions is really about. Not disrupting Hollywood. Not some tech platform. Just people — real people in real towns — building something together around the thing they love.

"We change everything. Not with protests or petitions. With a movie, a beer, and a conversation that lasts until they kick us out."

For the filmmaker who maxed out their credit card and poured everything into a story the world needs to hear. And for the Renegades in every town who show up for them — who fill a room, pass the word, and make a night of it.

Studios take 90 cents on every dollar. Algorithms decide what gets seen. We don't need them. We have each other.

No ratings. No thumbs. No algorithm.
Just cinema, community, and a good night out.
