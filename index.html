The Story

Indie Revolutions was built because the system was never built for the little guy. Independent filmmakers pour everything into their work — and then watch it die on a hard drive because they can't get a meeting in Hollywood.

We built a platform that matches indie films with the people in every American town who are hungry for exactly what those filmmakers made. No studios. No algorithms. Just cinema finding its people.

✊ The Renegade Code

There is
one rule.

We talk about movies and the positive things they bring into our lives. Nothing more. Nothing less.

The world outside is a minefield of negativity, judgement, and noise. Leave it at the door.

"We are warriors — for film, for culture, for each other. Every creed. Every background. Every person who has ever felt like the world wrote them off."

We don't judge. We don't tear down. We lift up. If you've fallen — from grace, from hope, from belonging — there is a seat for you in the dark next to us.

We are Renegades.
All of us.
Together.

✊ Our Mission

Indie Revolutions
exists for the little guy.

For the filmmaker who maxed out their credit card, shot on weekends, and poured every piece of themselves into a story the world needs to hear — but can't get a meeting in Hollywood.

And for the Renegades. The people in every town who are done with sequels and algorithms. Who want cinema that punches them in the gut and changes how they see the world.

"No more being told what's cool by Hollywood or any corporation. We are not their audience. We are our own culture — built by real people, in real towns, around real stories."

We built Indie Revolutions because the system was never built for the little guy. Studios take 90 cents on every dollar. Algorithms decide what the world gets to see.

Entertain

Because joy, laughter, fear, and wonder are worth fighting for.

Influence

Because a single film can shift how a generation thinks.

Change the World

Because the little guys who bust their ass deserve to be heard.

That's the revolution.
Join it. ✊
